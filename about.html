This is the story of my lame life. I was born in Trieste, Italy in October 1998. I grew up watching America's Next Top Model and Project Runway, and this is why I started to love fashion. I always loved art and when I went to high school I took fashion design classes. In 2015 I went on an exchange year in the States and eventually it ended up being one of the best experiences of my life; the people I was staying with welcomed me as if I was part of the family. When I came back to Italy I was very upset and my parents decided to get a dog. Her name is Bella, and she is super cute. I later realized that the fashion field wasn't really for me, and when I graduated high school I decided that I wanted to become a graphic designer. I now live in Milan, Italy and I study at Milan's Politecnico.
